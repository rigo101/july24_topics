Adding SVG to your page

Read more: MDN - Adding SVG to your page

Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) can be rendered at any size without loss of quality. The SVG does not become pixelated as you zoom in and out.

SVGs are using algorithms — they contain shape and path definitions.

SVG icons are much lighter than their raster (png, jpg, gif) equivalents, because they don’t need to hold information on every pixel in the image individually.
